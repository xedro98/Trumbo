# Deep Codebase Intelligence

**Trembo starts from a structural view of your project and digs deeper only where it matters.**

Trembo never operates in a vacuum. It begins with a broad understanding of your repository, then runs targeted agentic exploration to pull in whatever specific context a task demands. The result is actions that are well-informed and consistent with your architecture, not guesses stitched together from a single open file.

Trembo is fully open source and self-hostable — bring your own API keys, run it anywhere, and keep full control of your workflow.
