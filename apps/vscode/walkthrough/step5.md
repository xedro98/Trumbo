# No Black Box: Full Visibility & Control

**Trembo works in the open — every file read and every proposed diff is visible.**

Nothing happens behind your back. Review each action, approve changes before they land, and roll back through checkpoints when a direction doesn't pan out. Because Trembo runs on your own keys, you also see exactly what each turn costs.

Trembo is fully open source and self-hostable — bring your own API keys, run it anywhere, and keep full control of your workflow.
